### Motivation 

Sometimes it's hard to be motivated by the same boulders over and over again. This is especially true if it feels like **there is nothing to work on** (e.g. the easy boulders to warm-up are too easy; the hard boulders are impossible or totally not your style). 

Of course, in a situation like this, it's still possible to find joy in climbing and progress. One just has to work a little bit harder for it.
